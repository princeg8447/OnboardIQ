# TechCorp Employee Handbook — HR Policies

## 1. Leave Policy

### Annual Leave
Full-time employees are entitled to 21 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
Leave accrues at 1.75 days per month. Unused leave can be carried over up to a maximum
of 10 days to the next calendar year. Leave beyond the carry-over limit will lapse.

### Sick Leave
Employees receive 12 days of paid sick leave per year. A medical certificate is required
for sick leave exceeding 3 consecutive days. Sick leave does not carry over to the next year.

### Casual Leave
6 days of casual leave are available per year for personal errands or emergencies.
Casual leave cannot be taken for more than 3 consecutive days. Prior approval from
the reporting manager is required.

### Maternity & Paternity Leave
- Maternity leave: 26 weeks (paid) for the first two children.
- Paternity leave: 15 days (paid) within 6 months of childbirth.

### Leave Application Process
1. Log in to the HR portal at hr.techcorp.internal
2. Navigate to Leave Management → Apply for Leave
3. Select leave type, dates, and add remarks
4. Submit for manager approval (minimum 3 days advance notice required)
5. You will receive an email confirmation once approved

## 2. Expense Reimbursement Policy

### Travel Reimbursement
Employees travelling for official purposes are eligible for reimbursement of:
- Flight/train tickets (economy class for domestic; business class for international flights >6 hrs)
- Hotel accommodation up to ₹6,000/night in metro cities, ₹4,000/night elsewhere
- Local conveyance (cab receipts required)
- Daily meal allowance: ₹800/day (domestic), $50/day (international)

### How to Claim Reimbursement
1. Collect all original receipts
2. Fill the expense claim form on the HR portal within 30 days of travel
3. Attach scanned receipts (PDF or JPG)
4. Submit to your manager for approval
5. Finance team processes approved claims within 7 working days

### Mobile & Internet Allowance
- Senior staff (L5+): ₹1,500/month mobile + ₹1,000/month internet
- All others: ₹800/month mobile + ₹600/month internet
- Reimbursed monthly via payroll

## 3. Benefits & Perks

### Health Insurance
- Coverage: Employee + spouse + 2 children (up to ₹5,00,000/year)
- Premium fully paid by TechCorp
- Covers hospitalization, day-care procedures, pre/post hospitalization (60/90 days)
- Enrolment happens automatically on joining date

### Life Insurance
- Coverage: 5x annual CTC
- Accidental disability: additional 5x annual CTC
- Nominated beneficiary receives payout

### Provident Fund (PF)
- 12% of basic salary contributed by employee
- 12% matched by TechCorp
- Managed via EPFO; UAN provided on Day 1

### Gratuity
- Eligible after 5 years of continuous service
- Formula: (Last drawn basic salary × 15 × years of service) / 26

### Employee Stock Options (ESOPs)
- Offered to eligible employees based on grade and performance
- Standard vesting: 4-year cliff with 1-year cliff
- Details shared by the Finance team at joining

## 4. Performance & Appraisal

### Review Cycle
- Mid-year check-in: July (informal, development-focused)
- Annual appraisal: January (formal rating + increment)
- Ratings: Exceptional / Exceeds Expectations / Meets Expectations / Below Expectations

### Goal Setting
Goals are set using the OKR framework. Employees must set objectives in the HR portal
within the first two weeks of each half-year cycle.

### Increment Policy
- Increments are effective 1st April each year
- Increment range: 0–30% depending on rating and market benchmarking
- Promotions may carry additional increment beyond the standard range

---

## 5. Code of Conduct

### Workplace Behaviour
All employees are expected to treat colleagues, customers, and partners with respect.
Discrimination, harassment, or bullying of any kind will not be tolerated and may
result in immediate termination.

### Confidentiality
Employees must not disclose proprietary information, client data, or internal strategies
to external parties. This obligation continues for 2 years post-employment.

### Conflict of Interest
Employees must disclose any outside business interests or investments that may conflict
with TechCorp's business to the HR department within 15 days of joining.
