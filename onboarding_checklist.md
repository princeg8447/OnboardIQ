# TechCorp New Employee Onboarding Guide

## Before Day 1 (Pre-joining)

- [ ] Accept offer letter via email link
- [ ] Complete background verification (BGV) — link sent to email
- [ ] Submit scanned documents: Aadhaar, PAN, last 3 months payslips, degree certificates
- [ ] Fill the pre-joining form on the HR portal
- [ ] Confirm reporting date and office location with HR

---

## Day 1 Checklist

### Morning (9:00 AM — Report to Reception)
- [ ] Collect access card from Security Desk (carry a government photo ID)
- [ ] Meet your HR buddy — they'll guide you through the day
- [ ] Attend new joiner orientation session (10 AM, Conference Room B)

### IT Setup
- [ ] Collect your laptop from the IT desk (Floor 3)
- [ ] Raise an IT ticket at it-helpdesk.techcorp.internal for:
  - Email account activation
  - VPN access
  - Slack workspace invite
  - GitHub/GitLab access (for engineering roles)
  - Jira/Confluence access
- [ ] Set up MFA on your corporate email (mandatory)
- [ ] Install required tools from the Software Portal

### HR Paperwork
- [ ] Sign employment contract (physical copy)
- [ ] Submit original educational certificates for verification (returned within 5 days)
- [ ] Fill bank account details for salary processing
- [ ] Submit PF nomination form
- [ ] Enrol for health insurance (complete the dependent declaration form)

---

## Week 1 Checklist

### Know Your Team
- [ ] 1:1 with your direct manager (Day 1 or 2)
- [ ] Introduction call with your skip-level manager (by end of week)
- [ ] Join team standup / sync calls
- [ ] Get added to all relevant Slack channels

### Set Up Your Tools
- [ ] Complete email and calendar setup
- [ ] Set your work hours and OOO settings in Google Calendar
- [ ] Access the internal wiki on Confluence
- [ ] Review team's Jira board and current sprint

### Mandatory Training (complete within first 5 days)
- [ ] Information Security Awareness (45 mins) — LMS link sent to email
- [ ] POSH (Prevention of Sexual Harassment) Training (30 mins)
- [ ] Code of Conduct Acknowledgement (sign and submit to HR)
- [ ] Data Privacy & GDPR Basics (for roles handling client data)

### HR Portal Setup
- [ ] Log in to HR portal: hr.techcorp.internal
- [ ] Verify personal details and emergency contact
- [ ] Download salary slip access instructions
- [ ] Activate your UAN (PF) account

---

## Month 1 Goals

- Complete your 30-day onboarding plan with your manager
- Set OKRs for your first half-year cycle
- Complete department-specific onboarding sessions
- Shadow at least 2 client/stakeholder calls (client-facing roles)
- Review the team's technical documentation / architecture docs (engineering)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**When will I receive my first salary?**
Salary is processed on the last working day of each month. If you join after the 15th,
your first month's salary will be pro-rated and paid the following month.

**How do I book a meeting room?**
Use Google Calendar → Create Event → Add a room. All meeting rooms are listed there.
For large events (>20 people), email admin@techcorp.com with 48 hours notice.

**Who is my HR Business Partner (HRBP)?**
Your HRBP is assigned based on your department. Check the HR portal under "My HR Team"
or ask in Slack #hr-helpdesk.

**What is the work-from-home (WFH) policy?**
TechCorp follows a hybrid model: minimum 3 days in office per week (Tuesday, Wednesday,
Thursday are mandatory in-office days). WFH is allowed on Monday and Friday.
Extended WFH requests (>1 week) require manager + HR approval.

**How do I access the company VPN?**
After IT sets up your account, download the Cisco AnyConnect client. Credentials will
be emailed to your corporate email. VPN is required for accessing internal systems
outside the office.
